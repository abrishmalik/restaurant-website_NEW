About Our Resturant

The breakfast menu features pancakes made from scratch, fluffy omelets, waffles with fruit salsa and fresh roasted Mad River coffee. The lunch menu serves up creative salads, wraps, sandwiches & fresh burgers. The dinner menu offers diners items such as flat bread pizzas, goat cheese and beet salad with steak tips, chipotle alfredo shrimp linguini and bbq steak burrito. A full kid’s menu is also available. The Bay Restaurant also provides free WIFI and leather chairs to relax and enjoy a cup of cappuccino with a pastry.
